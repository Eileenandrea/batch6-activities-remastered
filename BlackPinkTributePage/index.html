About Blackpink

BLACKPINK captivated the public as soon as they made debut back in 2016 through ‘SQUARE ONE’ and heralded the birth of a major rookie group. The group had released mini albums ‘SQUARE UP’ and ‘Kill This Love’ and grew up as global artists. BLACKPINK proved their global popularity by a successful world tour and performing at ‘Coachella’, the biggest music festival in the United States. They’ve made comeback this year on June with their charismatic pre-release single ‘How You Like That’, followed up by the release of ‘Ice Cream’, the song full of unexpected charms, which ranked at no. 13 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. After proving their global influence, BLACKPINK will make a new challenge by releasing their official debut album on October 2nd.
